Metaphysics Θ (Theta) · Ι (Iota) · Κ (Kappa) · Λ (Lambda)

Being as actuality: potentiality and actuality, unity and its relatives, the compendium, the Unmoved Mover

The third tetralemmic position — the answer given. Theta analyses the actuality/potentiality distinction that Eta showed to be fundamental. Iota investigates unity and the one. Kappa recapitulates and bridges to natural philosophy. Lambda is the culmination: the Unmoved Mover as pure actuality, the ground of all being and motion, structurally identical to Buddhist unconditioned and the unnameable Tao.

Click any chapter to expand. Position III is the positive answer to the question posed in Position I and determined in Position II. Theta shows that being is fundamentally actuality — the realisation of potentiality. Iota analyses unity as the measure of being. Kappa consolidates the results. Lambda brings the entire inquiry to its limit: pure actuality, self-thinking thought, the Unmoved Mover — what being most fully is.
