Subject: the property — topoi for establishing or refuting that something is or is not the unique property of a species

Click any chapter to expand. Book V treats property (ἴδιον) — what belongs to a species alone, to all of it, and always, but is not its essence. The property converts with the species: if S has property P then P belongs only to S, and if P belongs to something then it is S. The topoi here test this convertibility from multiple angles.
