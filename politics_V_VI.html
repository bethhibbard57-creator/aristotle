Book V is among the most philosophically rich books of the Politics — it analyses the causes of constitutional change and revolution using the same four-cause framework that governs natural philosophy and metaphysics. Constitutional change is the political form of the natural change analysed in the Physics and GC. Book VI examines how the various kinds of democracy and oligarchy should organise their offices and institutions — the practical engineering of constitutional stability.
