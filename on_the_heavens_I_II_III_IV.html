Click any chapter to expand. On the Heavens extends Physics VIII into cosmology. Book I establishes the fifth element — aether — and the eternity of the heavens. Book II examines the structure and motions of the celestial spheres. Books III–IV treat the four sublunary elements, their natural places, and the properties of heaviness and lightness. Together the four books give the complete physical cosmology that GC II and Metaphysics Lambda presuppose.
