The boundary of psychology and biology — the physical basis of the soul's activities

Click any section to expand. The remaining Parva Naturalia extend the analyses of De Anima and the biological works into specific phenomena at the boundary of soul and body. On Sense deepens the account of perception from DA II. On Sleep, Dreams, and Divination analyse the suspension and continuation of soul-activities in sleep. On Length of Life, Youth and Old Age, and Respiration analyse the vital processes that sustain and terminate life. Together they complete the biological analysis of the soul's embodied existence.
