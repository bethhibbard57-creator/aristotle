Movement of Animals (11 chapters) & Progression of Animals (19 chapters) — complete interactive reference

How the soul moves the body · the mechanics of locomotion · the universal structural principles of animal movement

Click any section to expand. These two short works complete the biological analysis of locomotion. Movement of Animals analyses the causal mechanism by which the soul produces motion in the body — the bridge between psychology and biology. Progression of Animals analyses the structural principles governing how animals actually move — why they have the number of limbs they do, why the joints bend the way they do, and what the universal conditions of locomotion are. Together they show the same four-cause framework operating at the most concrete level of biological analysis.
