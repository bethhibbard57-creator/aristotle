The Porphyrian tree imposes strict binary division at every node. The dashed nodes are the logical scaffolding required to achieve this — they represent the differentia at work but are not named categories in Aristotle's text. Aristotle gives the ten categories as a list, not a tree. This diagram makes the underlying binary logic of their descent from Being explicit.
