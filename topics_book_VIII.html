Topics Book VIII — complete interactive reference · chapters 1–14

Subject: the practice of dialectical exchange — how to question, how to respond, how to manage the argument

Click any chapter to expand. Book VIII is the practical culmination of the Topics — it moves from the theory of topoi to the conduct of live dialectical exchange. It treats the questioner's strategy, the respondent's strategy, how to handle induction, how to manage concessions, what counts as victory or defeat, and the proper aims of dialectical argument. The elenchus as practiced by Socrates is the subject throughout.
