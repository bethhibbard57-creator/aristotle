The analysis of fallacious argument: thirteen modes of sophistic refutation, their sources, their detection, and their dissolution

Click any chapter to expand. The Sophistical Refutations is the concluding work of the Organon and the logical companion to the Topics. It systematically analyses every form of apparently valid but actually invalid argument — the thirteen fallacies — showing in each case where the appearance of validity comes from and how it is dissolved. Aristotle explicitly closes by noting this text completes the entire logical project.
